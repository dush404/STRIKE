Our Learning Roadmap

At STRIKE, we believe in a clear, structured path to mastery. Our learning roadmap is designed to guide you from foundational concepts to advanced specializations, ensuring you gain practical skills and confidence at every step.

Whether you're starting with Data Structures & Algorithms, diving into Generative AI, or preparing for top-tier interviews, our curriculum is meticulously crafted to accelerate your growth. Below is a general overview of the journey you can expect with STRIKE.

Stages of Your Journey

Phase 1: Building the Foundation

1

Programming Fundamentals

Building the Base

Master a core programming language (Python/C++). Learn variables, control flow, functions, and basic syntax.

2

Core Data Structures

Organizing Information

Implement and utilize fundamental data structures: Arrays, Linked Lists, Stacks, Queues, and Hash Tables.

3

Advanced Data Structures

Complex Data Handling

Explore Trees (BST, AVL, Red-Black), Tries, Graphs, and Heaps for advanced problem-solving.

4

Object-Oriented Programming

Modular Design

Understand OOP principles: Encapsulation, Inheritance, Polymorphism, and Abstraction for scalable code.

Phase 2: Mastering Algorithms

5

Algorithmic Techniques

Thinking Critically

Dive into Sorting, Searching, Recursion, Backtracking, Greedy Algorithms, and Dynamic Programming.

6

System Design Fundamentals

Scalable Solutions

Learn about distributed systems, databases, caching, load balancing, and microservices architecture.

Phase 3: Career Launchpad

7

Specialization & Projects

Build & Innovate

Choose a path (AI, Web Dev) and apply skills to build impressive, real-world portfolio projects.

8

Interview Preparation

Getting Job-Ready

Engage in mock interviews, resume building, and portfolio reviews with industry experts.
