About STRIKE

STRIKE was founded with a singular mission: to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry demands. We believe in learning by doing. Our platform is built on a foundation of project-based learning, mentorship from industry veterans, and a curriculum that is relentlessly focused on the skills that matter.

From mastering Data Structures & Algorithms to exploring the frontiers of Generative AI, our courses are designed to make you not just job-ready, but a standout candidate for the world's top tech companies. We provide the tools, the guidance, and the community; you bring the ambition.
